Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

This is a free, non‑commercial site. We do not run ads and use Google Analytics only to understand aggregate usage.

What We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect pseudonymous usage data, including pages visited, referrers, approximate location (based on IP), device/browser type, and interaction events. IP addresses are processed by Google and may be anonymized according to your region and Google’s settings.

How We Use Information

We use analytics data to understand site usage and improve features and performance. We do not sell personal information.

Minors

This site is intended for individuals aged 18 and older and is not directed to children or minors. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 18. If you believe a minor has provided information via this site, please clear the browser’s cookies/site data and use Google’s tools referenced below to manage analytics identifiers.

Cookies

We use cookies for essential site functionality and analytics. Analytics cookies may be set when you use the site. You can manage cookies via your browser settings or use the opt-out options below.

Your Rights

We do not maintain user accounts, mailing lists, or a server‑side database. Analytics data is handled by Google Analytics. If you are in a region with data protection laws (e.g., EU/UK) and wish to access, correct, or delete analytics data, or object to processing, please use Google’s privacy tools (see Google Privacy Policy) and the GA Opt‑out Add‑on.

For data stored locally in your browser (cookies/localStorage), you can clear site data in your browser settings. You may contact us for guidance, but we cannot directly act on Google’s analytics systems on your behalf.

Data Processors

Google acts as our analytics provider. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy and partner sites policy.

Data Retention

Retention of analytics data is controlled by Google Analytics. We do not control or shorten Google’s retention on your behalf.

Data Access and Deletion

Analytics data is collected and stored by Google Analytics. We do not control Google’s analytics databases, cannot identify your specific analytics record, and cannot disable, retrieve, or delete Google Analytics data on your behalf.

To manage data associated with your device/browser, use Google’s privacy tools (see Google Privacy Policy) or install the Google Analytics Opt‑out Browser Add‑on. You may also clear cookies and site data in your browser to reset identifiers.

Opt-Out

You can reduce or block analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on and/or adjusting your browser’s privacy settings to block or delete cookies.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
