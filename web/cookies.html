Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. We use cookies to enable essential functionality and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential: Required for basic site operation (e.g., remembering preferences).
  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies to measure usage and improve the site.
  • Advertising: Not used on this site.

Managing Cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.

For Google Analytics, you can also use the Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
